# Contributing

Thanks for helping grow the list. This repo is a curated index. The recipes themselves live on [FlowStacks](https://flowstacks.xyz).

## The fast path (recommended)

**[Submit your workflow on FlowStacks →](https://flowstacks.xyz/submit)**

Tell us what it does and how to reproduce it. If it passes verification, it gets:

- a permanent page with its full stack, cost, and verification tier,
- a badge (`✓ CI-verified` once it's wired into CI, `author-tested` before that),
- an entry on this list.

This is the best route because it gets your workflow *verified*, not just listed.

## Suggesting an entry by issue

Spotted a great workflow that belongs here? [Open an issue](../../issues/new) with:

- a one-line description of what it does,
- the stack (tools used),
- a link to where it's documented.

We'll review it, and if it's a fit, get it verified and added.

## What we look for

- **Reproducible.** Someone else can follow it and get the same result.
- **Specific.** A concrete outcome, not "use AI to be productive."
- **Honest about cost and limits.** What it needs, what it replaces, what it can't do.

## What this repo is not

- Not a place to paste full recipes (those live on FlowStacks, where they can be verified).
- Not a link farm. Every entry must point to a real, working workflow.
